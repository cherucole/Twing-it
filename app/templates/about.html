Twing It is a data analytics startup company headquartered and registered in Nairobi, Kenya.
We are a team of high tech professionals with vast experience in the African market implementing data analytics solutions.
We develop data analytics solutions for companies which have data but insuficient capacity to generate insights.

The Need for data analytics Since 2010, business are caught up in the big data buzz. We are all collecting more data than ever before.
Unfortunately, businesses lack the ability to convert data into meaningful insights!
While engaging industrial partners in Kenya, We have realized the growing demand for seamless availability and utilization of data to drive business perfomance. Due to this need, we are growing wings by creating awareness, replicating utilization of RDBMS, big data, Data mining and data visualization in all business sectors. We are a data analytics service provider with the right mix of data science expertise to support business build winning data strategies.
Skillset
Our services are supported by a strong skillset relevant for laying a data management strategy in any industry. Our ever growing set of tools and technologies includes, Python & R,Tableau & PowerBI, SQL & PLSQL, Talend & Dataiku, Ms Excel, Postgres, Mysql & SQL Server, D3.JS, Tensorflow etc.
Visual Analytics
